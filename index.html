          Optimization notes
          

            
Fast path: text-layer PDF → line scoring → evidence windows → rules or small AI chunks. This avoids sending the whole PDF to the model.

            
Hybrid mode: rules detect candidates first, then AI normalizes only the relevant context.

            
Backend upgrade path: replace browser WebLLM with a local FastAPI/Ollama endpoint, add OCR worker, store normalized extraction jobs in SQLite/Postgres, and stream results over SSE.
